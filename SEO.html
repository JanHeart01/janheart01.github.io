SEO

Search Engine Optimization (SEO) are rules followed by website (or blog) owners to optimize websites for search engines, and improve their search engine rankings. SEO is important in today's competitive market as search engines serve millions of users per day as they look for answers to their questions and solutions to their problems. SEO is responsible for businesses to grow and meet their objectives. As stated in the article, "What is search engine optimization and why is it important" The Importance of SEO:

  • The majority of search engines users are more likely to choose one of the top 5 suggestions in the results page so to take advantage of this and gain visitors to your web site or customers to your on-line store you need to rank as higher as possible.
  • It is not only about search engines but good SEO practices improve the user experience and usability of a web site.
  • User’s trust search engines and having a presence in the top positions for the keywords the user is searching increases the web site’s trust.
  • It is also good for the social promotion of your web site. People who find your web site by searching Google or Yahoo are more likely to promote it on Facebook, Twitter, Google+ or other social media channels.
  • SEO is also important for the smooth running of a big web site. Web sites with more than one author can benefit from SEO in a direct and indirect way. Their direct benefit is increase in search engine traffic and their indirect benefit is having a common framework (checklists) to use before publishing content on the site.
  • It can also put you ahead of the competition. If two web sites are selling the same thing, the search engine optimized web site is more likely to have more customers and make more sales.
  • SEO is a way to improve websites so they can appear in the top positions in the search results of search engines such as Google, Yahoo!, Bing, etc. When a search is performed, the order of the results are displayed as they are based on complex algorithms. They take a number of factors to decide the website/blog that will be shown first, second, and so on.
